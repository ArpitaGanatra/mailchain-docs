1.  You can now register your NEAR account with Mailchain.
    To do this, click 'Verify Address'

    :::note advanced option

    By default, Mailchain is able to receive messages for your existing blockchain wallet address before you sign up. If you do not wish to retrieve these messages, un-check the option to 'Retrieve messages sent to my address prior to registering'.

    :::

    <img
    	className="guides-image"
    	alt="verify address"
    	src="/img/user/guides/getting-started/img-register-a-wallet/verify-address-near.png"
    />

1.  A transaction request will appear in your wallet. Click 'More Information' to verify the contract information and transaction details.

    Verify the following Contract Details:

    **For Contract:** `messaging-keys.mailchain.near` <br/>
    **Function:** `set_messaging_key`

    Review the fees and the request then click '‚Üê' to go back to the transaction approval.

1.  After reviewing the transaction, you can 'Approve' it.

    <img
    	className="guides-image"
    	alt="transaction approval"
    	src="/img/user/guides/getting-started/img-register-a-wallet/transaction-approval-near.png"
    />

Your wallet is now verified.
