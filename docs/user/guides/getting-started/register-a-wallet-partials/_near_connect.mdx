Follow these steps to connect and register an existing blockchain wallet address:

1.  Once you have signed into Mailchain, head to your Inbox (https://app.mailchain.com/inbox)

1.  Click 'Register your wallet'

    <img
    	className="guides-image"
    	alt="register a wallet"
    	src="/img/user/guides/getting-started/img-register-a-wallet/register-your-wallet.png"
    />

1.  Select NEAR wallets

    <img
    	className="guides-image"
    	alt="select tezos"
    	src="/img/user/guides/getting-started/img-register-a-wallet/select-provider-near.png"
    />

1.  Click 'Connect'

    <img
    	className="guides-image"
    	alt="connect your wallet"
    	src="/img/user/guides/getting-started/img-register-a-wallet/connect-your-wallet-near.png"
    />

1.  Choose a wallet provider which supports the wallet address you are registering (this example uses MyNearWallet)

    <img
    	className="guides-image"
    	alt="select your wallet provider"
    	src="/img/user/guides/getting-started/img-register-a-wallet/select-your-wallet-provider-near.png"
    />

1.  On the Connect With NEAR, click 'Next' to confirm NEAR account you wish to register with Mailchain

    <img
    	className="guides-image"
    	alt="connect with NEAR screen"
    	src="/img/user/guides/getting-started/img-register-a-wallet/connect-with-near.png"
    />

1.  Click 'Connect' to allow Mailchain to read your address

    <img
    	className="guides-image"
    	alt="click connect button"
    	src="/img/user/guides/getting-started/img-register-a-wallet/click-connect-button-near.png"
    />

Your wallet is now connected.
